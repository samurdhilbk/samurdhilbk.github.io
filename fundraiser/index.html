Why we need funds?

AI/ML projects are still growing

With advances in AI and Machine Learning (ML) ever-expanding, more and more student & faculty-led research projects utilize some avenue of deep learning (DL).

GPU resources are outdated

The computer eng. department still only has an NVIDIA Tesla C2075 GPU and an NVIDIA Tesla K40 GPU; these extremely outdated GPUs are barely able to keep up with the demands of today's complicated (and large) DL models.

Compromises are made

Students & faculty have had to make do with free tools like Google Colab which only offers older GPUs in the free tier and has restrictions like time-limits for sessions.

FAQ

Is the usage of purchased equipment restricted to Pera computer/electrical engineering departments?

No. While the equipment will be housed in a server maintained by the Department of Computer Engineering, access will be granted to students and/or faculty of any other department (or Faculty) upon request.

How can I contact the organizers?

Samurdhi

How will the purchase and delivery of the equipment take place?

Upon the completion of Milestone 1, Samurdhi will purchase the NVIDIA RTX 3090 Ti GPU. If there is more time left in the fundraiser, it will be held to see whether Milestone 2 or Milestone 3 can be reached; if Milestone 2/Milestone 3 is reached, the rest of the parts will be purchased accordingly. If Milestone 1 is not reached within 30 days, the fundraiser will be renewed for another 30 days.

The purchased equipment will be hand-delivered to the department by one or more US-based alumni traveling to Sri Lanka before the end of 2022.

How much should I donate?

However much you can! There is no minimum donation, so donate within your means.

What will happen to the any excess donations (if any)?

Depending on the amount of excess donations, they may be:

Used to acquire additional upgrades such as RAM, storage, cooling etc.
Donated to the donated to the department to be used at their discretion

Before any of these actions are taken, all donors will be notified in advance.
