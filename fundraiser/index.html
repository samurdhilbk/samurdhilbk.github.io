Why we need funds?

AI/ML projects are still growing

With advances in AI and Machine Learning (ML) ever-expanding, more and more student & faculty-led research projects utilize some avenue of deep learning (DL). In fact 11 (out of 20 total) 2022 final year projects utilize ML.

GPU resources are outdated

The department still only has an NVIDIA Tesla C2075 GPU and an NVIDIA Tesla K40 GPU; these extremely outdated GPUs are barely able to keep up with the demands of today's complicated (and large) DL models.

Compromises are made

Students & faculty have had to make do with free tools like Google Colab which only offers older GPUs in the free tier and has restrictions like time-limits for sessions.

How will the purchase and delivery of the equipment take place?

Upon the completion of Milestone 1, Samurdhi will purchase the NVIDIA RTX 3090 Ti GPU. If there is more time left in the fundraiser, it will be held to see whether Milestone 2 or Milestone 3 can be reached; if Milestone 2/Milestone 3 is reached, the rest of the parts will be purchased accordingly. If Milestone 1 is not reached within 30 days, the fundraiser will be renewed for another 30 days.

The purchased equipment will be hand-delivered to the department by one or more US-based alumni traveling to Sri Lanka before the end of 2022.
